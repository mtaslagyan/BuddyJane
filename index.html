Your Business



Your business can use Buddy Jane to connect with the community, offer deals to drive traffic and expand your customer base.
With an Advanced Business Account:


  • Customers can place an order with you directly through Buddy Jane.
  • Upload and update your menu.
  • Access analytics to view sales patterns and forecast trends.
  • Directly target market your audience.
  • Build your network base with access to vendors near you.
